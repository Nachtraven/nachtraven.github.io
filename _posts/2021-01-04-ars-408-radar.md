# Continental ARS 408
## Exploring vehicular radars for railway vehicles

Increasing safety in motor vehicles has been a key focus for vehicle manufacturers these past years. A central element to this has been utilising ready made solutions for automatic braking and cruise control, such as this radar by continental.

Radar turned out to be too low resolution and provided no vertical information.
When LiDAR is available both cheaper and with better software, it was deemed not useful to pursue radar further.
